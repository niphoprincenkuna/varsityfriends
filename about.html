About Varsity Friends

Enlightening futures since 2022. We're on a mission to transform educational access for South African learners through simplified university applications.

Our Story

Varsity Friends was born from a simple observation: too many talented South African learners were missing out on tertiary education opportunities because the application process was complex, overwhelming, and poorly understood.

In 2022, three passionate individuals—Emmanuel Sepato, Bennet Sibiya, and Bongani Mkhabela—came together with a shared vision: to bridge the gap between ambitious learners and their academic dreams. What started as a small initiative to help local students has grown into a trusted educational support organization.

Today, we serve as a vital stepping stone for learners across South Africa, handling the paperwork, deadlines, and complexities of university applications so that students can focus on what matters most—their future.

Our Mission

To be South Africa's most trusted source of valuable, actionable information for tertiary education. We maximize admission opportunities by providing services that serve as stepping stones to personal transformation and academic success.

🔮

Our Vision

As we enlighten futures, we aim to establish accessible application centers, offices, and branches nationwide—improving our visibility, generating sustainable leads, and continuously improving the success rate of applications from our developing communities.

Our Values

  • Empowerment: Providing knowledge, resources, and support for informed decisions
  • Agency: Fostering self-reliance and personal growth
  • Accessibility: Breaking down barriers to educational opportunities
  • Integrity: Honest, transparent guidance in every interaction
  • Community: Building stronger futures together
