In Partnership With

A Powerful Collaboration for Educational Access

The Clementine Mahlwele Education Foundation collaborates with the Varsity Friends Organisation to support learners in accessing tertiary education opportunities through structured institutional and funding application support.

The partnership between the two organisations was formally established in 2022, with the shared objective of improving access to higher education for learners, particularly those from disadvantaged communities. Under this collaborative model, the Foundation mobilises resources and gathers donations to compensate the Varsity Friends Organisation for the professional application support services provided to learners.

The Foundation plays a key coordination and mobilisation role by liaising directly with school management teams, organising learner participation, and facilitating engagement platforms such as Grade 12 Career Expos. These career expos provide learners from multiple schools with access to critical career guidance, tertiary education information, and application support services. Through the Foundation's established relationships with schools, Varsity Friends Organisation is able to reach a broader base of learners across different institutions in a structured and organised manner.

The Varsity Friends Organisation provides the technical and administrative expertise required to facilitate applications to institutions of higher learning and student funding bodies. This includes assisting learners with career guidance, application submissions, documentation compliance, and tracking of application outcomes. Through the support facilitated by the Foundation, Varsity Friends Organisation is able to extend its services to a wider group of learners who may otherwise face barriers in accessing tertiary education opportunities.
