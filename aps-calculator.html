🎓 Bachelor's Pass

  • Minimum APS: 23 points
  • 40% in Home Language
  • 50% in 4 other subjects
  • 30% in 2 other subjects
  • Qualifies for: University Degree

📜 Diploma Pass

  • Minimum APS: 19 points
  • 40% in Home Language
  • 40% in 3 other subjects
  • 30% in 2 other subjects
  • Qualifies for: Diploma / Tech

📋 Higher Certificate

  • Minimum APS: 15 points
  • 40% in Home Language
  • 40% in 2 other subjects
  • 30% in 3 other subjects
  • Qualifies for: Certificate Course
